Searching For Hope

He held on to memories,
of their bond so strong,
But realized it was time to accept that they no longer belong.
With a heavy heart,
he let go of the past,
Knowing that the love was just lust
He was tired of smiling while holding back tears.
Though sadness lingered,
he found peace in release,
Understanding that sometimes,
it's better to find inner peace.
For in letting go,
he found a new start,
And embraced the hope of mending his broken heart.

Me In Disguise

My broken heart's never going to be healed
It fought and lost once before
Begged for a second chance,
but not anymore
Since then it's learned to close the door

Why hold on to a love that's no longer there?
She ain't going to love me anymore and I'm aware

So I walked into her room
But I was disguised
She thinks we've been apart for a while
But I was there the whole time

It's true it's me in disguise
I came to fulfil my last promise
Hoping to solve this crisis
